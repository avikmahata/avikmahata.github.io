Teaching

Courses taught in Mechanical and Electrical Engineering, focusing on computational modeling, system dynamics, and design.

MEN 4020A – Finite Element Analysis

Introduces fundamentals of finite element analysis (FEA) with MATLAB and ANSYS for solid mechanics and heat transfer problems, emphasizing numerical formulation and simulation-based design.

GEN 6111 – Engineering Analysis

Graduate-level analytical methods course covering vector spaces, PDEs, Green’s functions, and complex variables for advanced engineering applications.

View Syllabus (PDF)

ME 341 – Machine Design

Design of machine elements using Shigley’s approach, covering gears, shafts, bearings, and finite element applications. Delivered online with projects and presentations.
